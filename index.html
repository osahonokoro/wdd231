About Me

Hello! My name is Osahon Okoro, a passionate and driven student at Brigham Young University–Idaho, currently pursuing a degree in Web and Computing. I have a deep interest in building digital experiences that are not only functional but also intuitive and visually engaging.

My journey into web development began with a curiosity for how websites work, which quickly evolved into a commitment to mastering both front-end and back-end technologies. I am proficient in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Python, and I’ve worked on projects that span from static websites to dynamic, data-driven applications.

Beyond coding, I enjoy solving real-world problems through technology, collaborating with peers on team-based projects, and continuously learning new tools and frameworks. This portfolio showcases my academic progress, personal projects, and the skills I’ve developed along the way.

My goal is to become a well-rounded full-stack developer who contributes meaningfully to the tech community and builds solutions that make a difference. Thank you for visiting my portfolio!
